Kelvin Tan
Project Manager
MEng Civil Engineering
UCL(United Kingdom)

Alumni of the prestigious University College London (UCL) having graduated with MEng Civil Engineering under Gamuda Scholarship Program.

In Gamuda, Kelvin was part of the Geotechnical design team for Klang Valley Mass Rapid Transit (KVMRT), and his biggest project involved to date is the Penang Transport master plan in which he was the project engineer in charge of resources allocation, project budgeting and scheduling. With his vast experience in the construction industry complimented with the right attitude, Kelvin has able to hone and prove his skill in strategic planning and project management.
