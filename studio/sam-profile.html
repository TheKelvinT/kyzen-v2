Sam Wong
Technical Director
Dip. In Tech (Building)
TARC

An accomplished and seasoned name within the interior fit-out industry. Sam started his career as a Quantity Surveyor in BEP, one of the biggest architectural firm in the 80s.

As a young aspiring entrepreneur, he founded Astro Gain out of passion in his mid-twenties which specialized in interior fit-outs. Throughout the 30 years, he had built up an astonishing portfolio which includes some of his notable clients such as Royal Selangor, Versace, Yves Saint Laurent, Auntie Anne and so on .
