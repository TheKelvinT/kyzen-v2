About Us

The name Kyzen is derived from the Sino-Japanese word ‘改善‘, meaning to improve for the better. Having involved in the industry for a number of years we have faced many problems when dealing with typical contractors. Eg: poor workmanship, lack of management skill, poor paperwork (contracts & authority), Over charging clients and so on.... With a team of more than 25 years of hands-on experiencet, Kyzen Bina believes that have the capability to deliver high quality services that exceeds client needs.

What we Do

We are a boutique. design driven practice based In Petaling Jaya, Selangor. Malaysia, where we offer customised design solutions, tailor-made to suit our clients' needs and exceed their expectations! We believe every project is unique and the best results are obtained from intimate discourse and development with our clients. We are passionate about our work and we seek equally passionate clients who will willingly work closely with us to push the limits of what is possible.

We are a very versatile office, our projects range from Architecture to Interiors and in various categories: residential. commercial. food & beverage and hospitality. We seek to constantly improve and evolve, to keep up, and wishfully stayahead of current trendsand progress! We try to ensure that our projects are future-proofed and sustainable.

We are accountable and professional in our servicing and yet we strongly believe that the best works are produced when we are having fun!
